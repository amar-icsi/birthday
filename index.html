About Her ✨

Woh ek aisi ladki hai jo har pal apni muskaan se duniya ko roshan karti hai. Uski baatein, uska pyaar, sab kuch itna khubsurat hai ki dil ko sukoon milta hai. Uski har ek baat mein ek alag hi magic hai jo sabko apna bana leti hai. Uska hona meri zindagi ka sabse accha hissa hai, jo hamesha mujhe khush rakhta hai.

Our Story 📖

Shuruat mein hum bas ache dost the, dono apni zindagi mein khush the. Phir dheere-dheere, har pal mein ek naya pyaar, ek nayi khoobsurati dikhayi dene lagi. Jab hum ek dusre ke saath hote the, lagta tha jaise duniya ki har khushi mere paas aa gayi ho. Aaj hum yahan hain, aur main har pal tumhare saath jeena chahta hoon. Tum meri zindagi ka sabse khoobsurat hissa ho, aur main hamesha tumhare saath rahna chahta hoon!
